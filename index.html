VetriApp Privacy Policy v1.1

Effective Date: April 07, 2026

VetriApp is an offline-first business accounting and inventory management app. Your data stays on your device and is never sold or shared with advertisers.

1. Information We Collect

  • Business details (name, GSTIN, contact info)
  • Invoices, expenses, payments
  • Inventory and stock data
  • Customer and vendor information
  • Optional Google account (for backup)

We do NOT collect: location data, financial data on servers, or tracking analytics.

2. How We Use Data

  • Generate invoices and reports
  • Calculate GST
  • Track inventory
  • Enable encrypted backups

3. Data Storage & Security

  • Stored locally on your device
  • AES-256 encryption
  • Secure PIN protection
  • Encrypted Google Drive backup (optional)

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Drive (backup)
  • Google Play (billing)
  • Razorpay (future payments)

5. Your Rights

  • Access and export your data
  • Edit or delete data anytime
  • Disconnect backups

6. Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app.

7. Compliance

VetriApp complies with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP) 2023.

8. Changes

We may update this policy and notify users through the app.
